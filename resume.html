Simplified Resume

Overview

I am a team-oriented and hard working programmer that has found great interest in game developement.
In programming, i am particularly interested in developing AI, procedural generation and tools.
Although i have specialized in programming, i also work as a teacher.

Education

ETIC - Escola de Tecnologias Inovação e Criação (2018-2020)

    Ability to program C# at a fluent level.

    Proficiency in several important Unity Components(Animator, Canvas, Navmesh, etc...).

    Proficiency with Unity Engine.

    Understanding of web developement.

    Understanding of 3D modelling, texturing and animating.

    Knowledge of every step of game developement.

    Understanding of how to work in a team and how to work with version control software.

Professional Experiences

Teacher/Tutor at ETIC(2021-current)

    Organize and plan several lessons.

    Teaching Unity and C# (in and out of Unity).

    Planning of the Videogame Developement course and it's respective activities.

Programmer at Black Voyage Studios(2022-current)

    Responsible for the procedural generation of the hex game map.

    Utilized several algorithms to make a dynamic perlin based generation.

    Random encounters/events based on the tiles and the act that the player is in.

Programmer at Adamastor Studio(2021-2022)

    Refactored online synchronization systems.

    Responsible for the synchronization of the procedurally generated dungeons.

    Synchronization of several important game elements.

    Worked closely with the rest of the team.

Internship at DreamStudios(2021-2022)

    Lead programmer for the project "ViVo".

    Responsible for the integration of all assets (UI, animations, etc...).

    Dynamic UI through the use of layout groups, grids, etc...

    Developement of all systems in place (AI, skills, etc...).

    Developed a custom editor in order to aid the team designer with the creation of custom abilities.

    Developement of highly modular ability system.

Linguistic Capabilities

Portuguese - Native.

English - Advanced.

Spanish - Independent.

Skills

Strong knowledge of C#.

Strong knowledge of Unity Engine.

Ability to program VR & Mobile apps/games.

Strong knowledge of more recent systems in Unity, for instance, the new input system.

Capable of 3D modelling.

Soft Skills

Strong organization skills.

Great teamwork and communication.

Leadership skills.

Capable of working under pressure.

Other Activities

Consistent participation in several game jams.

VR/AR Course. (2022)

Speaker for ERASMUS project regarding Gamification. (2022)

Team lead for project EME 2022 at ETIC Algarve. (2022)

Representative at Lisbon Games Week. (2019)
