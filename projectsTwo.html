About me

Dice 'n Bullets

Project Overview

Dice 'n Bullets is a fast paced, doom-like first person shooter with some elmeents of luck thrown into the mix. The player is tasked with reaching the top of a tower that is littered with enemies. In order to reach his goal, the player must roll his dice to get a weapon (hopefully he gets a good one!).

My Role

As a programmer, i was tasked with implementing all of the gameplay systems and some of the VFX. One of the bigger challenges of this project was ensuring that there was a doom-like feel to it, which was achieved with a pixelated camera and smooth movement.

Colored Rails

Project Overview

This project was originally done as an exam with the duration of 7 days. However, i decided to polish several aspects. A hyper-casual mobile game where the player has to catch rails in order to teach the end of each level. There is the added twist that the player's pole only interacts with objects of the same color!

My Role

I was responsible for all of the 3D modelling (with the exception of the player character) and implementation of the UI, animations and game mechanics. I also implemented procedural level generation, although that was done after the exam was completed.
